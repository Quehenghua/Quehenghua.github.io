## 👋 About Me
majoring in Cybersecurity at Sichuan University.
I am currently a junior student at **Sichuan University**, majoring in double degree in Cybersecurity and Law. My academic interests include **content security**, **computer vision**, **deep learning**, and **multimodal understanding**.

My current research focuses on **Chinese harmful meme detection**, especially the detection and identification of harmful content distributed across visual and textual modalities. Beyond multimodal content safety, I am also interested in **deepfake detection** and **computer vision**, particularly in detecting AI-generated or manipulated images, and capturing subtle visual artifacts and cross-modal inconsistencies.
